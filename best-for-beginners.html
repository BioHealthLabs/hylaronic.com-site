Starter routine (the safe way)

  1. Cleanse with a mild, non-drying cleanser.
  2. Apply HA on slightly damp skin (never bone dry).
  3. Use 2–4 drops and spread evenly across the face.
  4. Follow with a moisturiser to “seal in” hydration.
  5. Use SPF 30+ every morning.

Common beginner mistakes

  • Skipping moisturiser: HA is a humectant, not a “seal”. Always lock it in.
  • Using too much: more serum often equals more stickiness/pilling.
  • Stacking multiple HA layers: one good serum is usually enough.
  • Expecting HA to replace actives: HA hydrates; it won’t replace SPF or retinoids.
