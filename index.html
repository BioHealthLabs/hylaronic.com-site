Hyaluronic Acid FAQ

Can hyaluronic acid make my skin drier?

It can feel that way if you apply it on completely dry skin and skip moisturiser. For best results, apply HA on slightly damp skin and follow with a moisturiser to lock water in.

Can I use hyaluronic acid every day?

Yes. Most people can use HA once or twice daily. If a product pills, feels sticky or breaks you out, it’s usually the overall formula or application amount — not HA itself.

Do I still need moisturiser if I use a hyaluronic serum?

Yes. HA is a humectant (water binder), not an occlusive. You still need a moisturiser on top to seal hydration in.
