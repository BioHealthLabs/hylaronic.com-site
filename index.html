How to choose the right hyaluronic serum

Hyaluronic acid is simple on paper ‚Äì it binds water ‚Äì but formulas vary a lot in feel and performance.

  • Dehydrated + oily: choose very lightweight, watery serums without heavy oils.
  • Dry or tight skin: go for cream-gel textures with ceramides and lipids.
  • Red, reactive skin: fragrance-free, alcohol-free and minimal INCI lists are best.

Common mistakes with hyaluronic acid

Hyaluronic acid is generally gentle ‚Äì but used wrong, it can feel sticky or even make dryness worse.

  • Applying on completely dry skin with no moisturiser on top.
  • Using too many layers of different HA serums in the same routine.
  • Relying on HA alone without any occlusive or creamy moisturiser to seal it in.

Can hyaluronic acid ever dry my skin out?

In almost all cases, yes. Hyaluronic acid is a humectant ‚Äì it attracts water ‚Äì but you still need emollients and occlusives from a moisturiser to keep that water in the skin.
