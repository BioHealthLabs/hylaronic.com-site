Hyaluronic Acid FAQ

Can hyaluronic acid make my skin drier?

It can feel that way if you apply it on completely dry skin and skip moisturiser. For best results, apply HA on slightly damp skin and always follow with a cream to lock in water.

Can I use hyaluronic acid every day?

Yes – most people can use hyaluronic serums once or twice daily. If a product feels sticky, pills or causes breakouts, it’s usually the overall formula, not HA itself.

Do I still need moisturiser if I use a hyaluronic serum?

Yes. Hyaluronic acid is a humectant (water binder), not an occlusive. You still need a moisturiser on top to seal the hydration into the skin.
